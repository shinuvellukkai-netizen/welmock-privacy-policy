Privacy Policy for WELMOCK

WELMOCK collects only the information required to provide mock tests, scheduled exams, and result tracking.

Information We Collect

  • Google account basic profile information such as name and email address for authentication
  • Admission number and role information such as student or instructor
  • Exam activity including test attempts, answers, scores, and submission history

How We Use Information

  • To authenticate users
  • To provide role-based access for students and instructors
  • To schedule exams and allow students to join exams
  • To store exam results and progress history
  • To review submissions and performance
  • To monitor app performance, analytics, and crash reports for reliability improvements

Data Storage

User and exam data are stored using Firebase services configured by the app owner.

Data Sharing

WELMOCK does not sell personal data. Data is used only for app functionality and academic or exam management within the app.

Data Retention

Data is retained as needed for app functionality, exam review, and progress history unless deletion is requested by the app owner or user through support, or the user deletes their account from within the app.

User Rights

Users may request correction or deletion of their profile or exam-related data through the support contact, and signed-in users can also delete their account directly from the app.

Security

Reasonable steps are taken to protect stored data, but no online system can guarantee absolute security.

Children

This app is intended for educational and exam preparation use. If required by local policy, use should be supervised by the institution or guardian.
